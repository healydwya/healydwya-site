Our Story

When using IBM's Design Thinking framework to identify a need in our world, Vanessa told a story about her younger sister having trouble staying interested in learning over summer break. Hence, the idea for an education platform was born.

We didn't want to stop here though, as we had a vision of improving the world for the better. What we came up with was a web-based application with incentives through company discounts for people young and old to learn more about their communities and world.
